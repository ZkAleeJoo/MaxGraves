# MaxGraves
MaxGraves is a plugin that allows you to save your items in case you die. It has these features:

## Features
- When you die, a grave is generated where your items and experience will be stored.
- With just one click you can recover all your things, the armor or items you have in your second hand will be automatically equipped.
- When you die you can execute the **/mg info** command to see a menu with all your active graves.
- When you die you will be given a map, which by right-clicking will teleport you to your grave, this teleport is managed under the **maxgraves.tp** permission.
- Your graves accumulate depending on how many deaths you have, the duration of these can be managed from **config.yml**.

## Permissions and Commands

- /maxgraves reload - maxgrave.admin
- /maxgraves info - maxgrave.info - default **true**
- /maxgraves tp - maxgrave.tp - default **false**
